About HNCO

About H&C Group of companies

We, looking at the growing needs of natural product industry for undiluted ingredients, started Herbs And Crops Overseas budding enterprise in 2013.

We at Herbs And Crops Overseas, focus on manufacturing, supplying and exporting world class quality natural and organic certified herbs. Our herbs from different categories are very widely used in domestic as well as international markets by various companies dealing in natural products for their formulations.

Our main focus goes to the companies which are willing deeply to provide the 100% Natural products to the consumers by adding a value, which the people are looking in the natural products. Let it be a Natural Cosmetic, or Effective Ayurvedic formulation, a food supplement or any other natural product, our motto is to always be with the formulators to get the final consumers:

" A product which is made with the Ingredient, which is 100% Natural, Pure and Undiluted. "

Who we are ?

  • Started in 2013 As an exporter dealing in natural herbs used for hair care product formulations.
  • We had over main focus on sourcing and exporting highest quality of herbs to the manufacturers and formulators of natural products.
  • With a root level experience of over 6 years, now we have expanded in multi-folds, serving to more than 7 industries, with our wide range of products for different applications.
  • With a successful track record and expertise in the natural product industry, we are now among the most emerging manufacturer and exporters from India in the Herbal Industry.
