specialise in manufacturing, supplying and exporting wide range of Natural and Organic Products to Health, Personal Care, Pharmaceutical and Various other industries.

Started in 2013

  • As an exporter dealing in natural herbs used for hair care product formulations.
  • We had over main focus on sourcing and exporting highest quality of herbs to the manufacturers and formulators of natural products.
